Momos

Momos, originating from Tibet is a hugely popular street food in the country, largely in North-India. A versatile food that it is, we have a wide variety available from vegetable, mutton to what we have here, chicken momos. With these momos the possibilities are literally endless.

Ingredients

  • chicken mince
  • frozen pastry from the chinese store
  • onion

Recipe

  1. mix chicken mince, onion and assortment of spices
  2. put the mix in the pastry and fold it
  3. steam the folded momos using a steamer
  4. Enjoy!
